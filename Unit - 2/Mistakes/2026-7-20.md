# 英语学习纠错笔记（四）

范围：
从 **“I woke up at 6 a.m., but got up still 10 a.m.”** 开始。

---

# 1. I woke up at 6 a.m., but got up still 10 a.m.

## 原句

> I woke up at 6 a.m., but got up still 10 a.m.

## 更自然表达

> I woke up at 6 a.m., but I didn’t get out of bed until 10 a.m.

---

## wake up 和 get up 的区别

这是一个很重要的区别。

### wake up

表示：

> 醒来

强调从睡眠状态变成清醒状态。

例：

> I woke up at 6 a.m.

我早上六点醒了。

---

### get up

表示：

> 起床，离开床

强调身体离开床。

例：

> I got up at 10 a.m.

我十点起床。

---

你的情况：

6点：
> woke up

10点：
> got up

所以：

> I woke up at 6 a.m., but I didn’t get up until 10 a.m.

---

# 2. got up still 10 a.m.

## 错误

> got up still 10 a.m.

---

## 正确表达

> got up at 10 a.m.

或者：

> didn’t get up until 10 a.m.

---

## until 的用法

表示：

> 直到……才……

结构：

> didn’t + 动词 + until + 时间

例如：

> I didn’t go to bed until 2 a.m.

我直到凌晨两点才睡。

> I didn’t start working until noon.

我直到中午才开始工作。

---

# 3. I lay on bed

## 原句

> I lay on bed.

## 更自然

> I lay in bed.

或者：

> I stayed in bed.

---

## in bed 和 on the bed

### in bed

表示：

人在床上休息、睡觉。

例如：

> I’m still in bed.

我还在床上。

---

### on the bed

表示：

物体或人在床的表面。

例如：

> My phone is on the bed.

我的手机在床上。

---

你的意思是：

醒了以后继续躺着。

所以：

> I stayed in bed.

最自然。

---

# 4. lay 的使用

你的句子：

> I lay in bed.

语法正确。

但是注意：

lay 有多个形式：

| 动词 | 意思 | 形式 |
|-|-|-|
| lie | 躺 | lie - lay - lain |
| lay | 放置 | lay - laid - laid |

表示“躺”：

现在：

> I lie in bed.

过去：

> I lay in bed.

过去分词：

> I have lain in bed.

---

# 5. review the mistakes

## 原句

> I review the mistakes

## 修改

> I reviewed the mistakes.

---

原因：

你描述的是已经发生的事情：

- woke up
- lay

所以后面的动作也应该使用过去式：

> reviewed

---

# 6. before some day

## 原句

> mistakes that I made before some day

## 不自然。

更自然：

> mistakes that I made a few days ago

或者：

> mistakes that I had made a few days earlier

---

## ago 和 earlier 的区别

### a few days ago

从现在看：

> 几天前

例：

> I made these mistakes a few days ago.

---

### a few days earlier

从过去某个时间看：

> 更早几天

例如：

> I reviewed mistakes that I had made a few days earlier.

时间顺序：

1. 犯错误
2. 几天后复盘错误

---

# 7. had made 的用法

## 句子

> I reviewed the mistakes that I had made a few days earlier.

---

## 为什么用 had made？

因为有两个过去动作：

更早：

> made mistakes

后来：

> reviewed mistakes

为了强调：

“过去的过去”

使用：

> had + 过去分词

---

结构：

> had made

= 已经做了

---

注意：

had made 不表示：

“之前从来没有复习过”。

它只表示：

动作发生顺序：

A 发生 → B 发生

---

# 8. I suddenly

## 原句

> I sudenly

## 正确：

> I suddenly

拼写：

suddenly

不是：

sudenly

---

## suddenly 的位置

通常：

> suddenly + 动词

例如：

> I suddenly woke up.

> I suddenly remembered something.

> I suddenly felt tired.

---

# 9. I suddenly want to watch a movie.

## 评价

正确。

更自然：

> I suddenly feel like watching a movie.

---

区别：

## want to do

表示：

想做某事。

> I want to watch a movie.

---

## feel like doing

表示：

突然有兴趣、有这个想法。

> I feel like watching a movie.

更符合：

“突然想看电影了”。

---

# 10. Is it natural there use the word suddenly?

## 原句

> Is it natural there use the word "suddenly"?

## 修改：

> Is it natural to use the word "suddenly" there?

---

## 问题：疑问句结构

英语不能：

> Is it natural there use...

需要：

> Is it natural to do something?

例如：

> Is it natural to say this?

> Is it natural to use this word?

---

# 11. The 空调 in my room 坏了

## 自然表达

> The AC in my room stopped working.

或者：

> The air conditioner in my room broke.

---

# 12. 刚刚安装了一个新的空调

## 中文思维：

> I installed a new AC.

---

如果你自己安装：

正确：

> I installed a new AC.

---

如果别人帮你安装：

更自然：

> I had a new AC installed.

---

# 13. have something done

重要结构：

> have + something + past participle

意思：

> 让别人完成某事

例：

> I had my computer repaired.

我让别人修电脑。

> I had my hair cut.

我去理发。

> I had a new AC installed.

我让别人安装了新的空调。

---

# 14. My cat fall many furs

## 原句

> My cat fall many furs.

## 正确：

> My cat sheds a lot of fur.

---

# 15. fall 和 shed

动物掉毛：

不用：

> fall

使用：

> shed

例：

> Cats shed fur.

猫掉毛。

---

# 16. many furs

错误：

> many furs

原因：

fur 通常不可数。

正确：

> a lot of fur

---

# 17. 吸猫毛

不要：

> suck cat fur

自然：

> vacuum up the cat hair

---

## remove 和 vacuum up

remove：

强调结果：

> remove the cat hair

清除猫毛。

vacuum up：

强调动作：

> vacuum up the cat hair

用吸尘器吸走猫毛。

---

# 18. I walked outside just now

## 更自然：

> I just went for a walk outside.

---

区别：

walk outside：

走到外面。

go for a walk：

去散步。

---

# 19. My feet is hurt

## 错误：

> My feet is hurt.

## 正确：

> My feet hurt.

---

原因：

feet 是复数。

foot:

> My foot hurts.

feet:

> My feet hurt.

---

# 20. I have woke up for a while

## 错误：

> I have woke up for a while.

## 正确：

> I have been awake for a while.

---

原因：

wake up 是动作。

awake 是状态。

“醒了一会儿”强调状态持续：

> have been awake

---

# 21. I don't know what I can do

语法正确。

但是：

> I don't know what to do.

更自然。

区别：

## what I can do

我能做什么。

强调能力。

## what to do

该做什么。

强调选择。

---

# 22. VR/VST problems

## 原句：

> I encountered some VR VST problems.

## 更自然：

> I ran into some VR and VST-related problems.

---

encounter：

正式。

run into：

聊天更自然。

---

# 23. I solved it for a one day

## 原句：

> I solved it for a one day.

## 正确：

> I spent the whole day solving them.

---

问题：

### it → them

因为：

> problems

是复数。

---

### spend 时间 doing

固定：

> spend time doing something

例如：

> I spent the whole day solving problems.

---

# 24. I am exsoured and tired

## 错误：

> exsoured

## 正确：

> exhausted

---

exhausted:

筋疲力尽。

比 tired 强。

所以：

> exhausted and tired

有重复。

更自然：

> I’m exhausted.

---

# 最终完整表达

你的几个事件可以这样说：

> I woke up at 6 a.m., but I didn’t get out of bed until 10 a.m. I stayed in bed and reviewed some mistakes I had made a few days earlier.

> My cat sheds a lot of fur every day, so I have to clean my clothes and bedsheets. I often use a vacuum cleaner to vacuum up the cat hair.

> I just went for a walk outside, and now my feet hurt. I feel tired.

> I ran into some VR and VST-related problems. I spent the whole day solving them, and now I’m exhausted.
